## Overview

The Forestreet API offers a comprehensive suite of data retrieval for market insights and companies within a market. Here's an overview of some of the data Forestreet provides:

- Real-time market discovery, or a selection of pre-built markets.
- The companies within those markets.
- Detailed company profiles including financials, ownership and shareholder, competitors, momentum scores, and services and features provided within that market.
- Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) metrics related to companies.
- Latest news and events at a market and individual company level.

With the Forestreet API, you will gain access to a rich set of market and company data, making it simple to derive market insights.
