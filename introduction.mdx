## Overview

The Forestreet API offers a comprehensive suite of data retrieval for market insights and companies within those markets. Here's an overview:

- Retrieve Markets
- Fetch Companies within Markets
- Individual Company Data including detailed profiles, financials, and other relevant data for specific companies.
- Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) metrics related to companies.
- News and Events

With the Forestreet API, developers gain access to a rich set of market, company, news and events, financial and ESG data, making it simpler to derive insights and integrate them into various platforms.
